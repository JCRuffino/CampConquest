Score the most points! You get 1 point per area you control, plus bonus points:

+1 for owning all the Birches · +1 for all the Willows · +1 for all the Oaks · +1 for both Glades (RPG + SD) · +1 for having the biggest connected group of touching areas (if teams tie for biggest, nobody gets it).

Areas that touch each other count as connected; areas separated by a hatched grey gap do not. Locked and unlocked areas are worth the same.

Instant win: if a team's score ever reaches 11 points — and bonus points count towards this — the game ends immediately and they win.

Every area's challenge is secret until your team presses Start Challenge Attempt at that area. Starting reveals the challenge — and commits your team: you must then record either a pass or a fail.

The app tells you who must hold the phone for this challenge — they read it aloud, and their teammate does it. Duty alternates every challenge.

Only one team at a time can be attempting any given area's challenge — while a team's attempt is in progress, that area is off-limits to everyone else until they record their pass or fail.

Some challenges are timed — and you won't know until you start! A countdown challenge gives you a fixed window that starts the moment you press Start; a count-up challenge races the clock, and your time is your result. The live timer shows in the area's popup on everyone's phone.

Only press Start when your team is actually at the area and ready to go.

Every challenge has a pass mark — reach it and you claim the area, recording your result (a number, time, distance…) which becomes the score other teams must beat to steal it from you.

Fall short of the pass mark and you must record a fail: your team is barred from that area's challenge for the rest of the game — no second chances, not even to steal it later.

Teams are on their honour — record claims, results and fails honestly!

Any claimed-but-unlocked area (shown hatched on the map) can be stolen: go there and start the same challenge — but you are not told the score to beat, only the challenge itself. Do your best, enter your result, and only then is the owner's score revealed: beat it and the area transfers to your team and locks (solid on the map, never stealable again).

A steal attempt is a duel that settles the area forever: the moment one rival team starts it, the third team is shut out of that area — and if your result doesn't beat the owner's (or you give up), the area locks for the original owner instead.

Until that duel starts, a claimed area is open to any rival team that hasn't already failed its challenge. So if one team failed it and another claimed it, only the third team can attempt the steal — and their attempt settles it for good.

So a weak result invites a steal, but every steal attempt permanently settles that area, one way or the other. Choose your battles!

Three teams of two players, one phone per team. When you join a team you enter both players' names.

Every challenge is done by one team member, and phone duty alternates with each challenge: the app names who must hold the phone — they read the challenge out loud, and their teammate is the one who attempts it.

Your live location is shared on the map so everyone can see where the action is. The game admin (unlocked with a password in Settings) can see all challenges, adjust the timer, fix mistakes on any area, and reset the game.

Instant win: reach 11 points at any moment — bonus points included — and the game ends on the spot; the app announces it.

Timer win: when the countdown ends, the highest score wins — areas owned plus bonus points (the full sets and the biggest connected group). Ties are broken by locked areas.

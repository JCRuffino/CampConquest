Take control of areas of the campsite. When the game timer runs out, the team controlling the most areas wins. If teams are tied, the team with more locked areas wins.

Every area has an initial challenge. Go to the area, complete its initial challenge as a team, then tap the area on the map and press Claim. It turns your team's colour and counts towards your score.

Teams are on their honour — only press Claim when you've genuinely completed the challenge!

Once you control an area you unlock its control challenge. Complete it to lock the area in — a locked area is yours for the rest of the game and can never be stolen.

But be careful: if you attempt the control challenge and fail, your team can never attempt that area's control challenge again. The area stays yours, but it remains open for other teams to steal.

Any area that is claimed but not locked can be stolen. Complete the area's initial challenge and press Steal — ownership transfers to your team.

After stealing, you can attempt the control challenge to lock it (unless your team already failed that area's control challenge earlier).

Three teams of two players. Join your team in Settings — your live location is then shared on the map so everyone can see where the action is.

Anyone with no team assigned is a spectator/admin: they can adjust the timer, fix mistakes on any area, and reset the game.

The game ends when the countdown hits zero. Most areas controlled wins; most locked areas breaks a tie. Locked and unlocked areas are otherwise worth the same.

Score the most points!

You get 1 point per area you control, plus bonus points:

+1 for owning the most Birches
+1 for the most Willows
+1 for the most Oaks
+1 for the most Glades
+1 for having the biggest connected group of touching areas

If any of these bonus point qualifiers end in ties then no one gets points for that bonus.

Areas that touch each other count as connected; areas separated by a hatched grey gap do not. Locked and unlocked areas are worth the same.

Instant win: if a team's score ever reaches 11 points the game ends immediately.

Area challenges are only revealed when you start that challenge attempt. You are immediately in the challenge as you read it.

All challenges are completed by one team member only, which alternates each challenge. Before a challenge starts the game will remind you which team member should be holding the phone and reading the challenge; the other team member is completing the challenge.

Some challenges will be on a timer — they might count down and you only have a certain amount of time to do the challenge, or time is recording and the faster you do the challenge the better. The type of challenge is only revealed when you start the challenge.

The live timer shows in the area's popup on everyone's phone.

Remember to only press Start when your team is actually at the area and ready to go.

Every challenge has a pass mark, which you have to beat to claim the area, recording your result (a number, time, distance…) which becomes the score other teams must beat to steal it from you.

Fall short of the pass mark and you must record a fail: your team is barred from that area's challenge for the rest of the game — you won't be able to attempt a steal later.

When a team claims an area the area will show as hatched in their colour.

The two other teams can now attempt to steal that area — once one team starts a steal attempt, the other team won't be able to. You must beat the current score to steal.

A steal locks the ownership for the rest of the game and the colour on the map will now be solid instead of hatched. If the steal is successful the new team now owns this area, and if the steal is unsuccessful the original team locks ownership.

There are two end game conditions.

Instant win: reach 11 total points and the game ends immediately; the app will announce this to all players.

Timer win: when the countdown ends, the highest score wins. Ties are broken by most locked areas, then most areas owned.
